About us

Sina Gerard/Ese URWIBUTSO is an international owned and managed by Mr. Sina Gerard. It is oriented towards agro-processing industries in sector. The company is located at Nyirangarama in Rulindo district, Nothern province about 45km from Kigali City, on Kigali-Musanze-Rubavu main road.
